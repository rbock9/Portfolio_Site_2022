FAQs:

Q: What's up with your website?

A: Thanks for visiting my portfolio website! You may have noticed it has a fairly "simple" look. This is because I challenged myself to design and build it without using any templates, libraries, or frameworks (excluding font and icon libraries), so what you see is just fundamental HTML, CSS, and JavaScript written by yours truly. I consider myself more of a developer than a designer, so I leaned on my desire to make a clean, simple, and efficient website adhering to the KISS principle rather than building something flashy, slow and impractical. That said, this site is an ongoing project, and I'll be sure to add and enhance its features and functionality over the coming months.

Q: How long have you been coding?

A: I first began learning basic HTML/CSS, web design and flash animation in high school (circa 2006). I later learned a bit more about web development and graphic design during an internship I landed with Custom Ink's Site Content team during winter 2015-2016. However, I didn't begin coding full-time until around December 2020. I spent most of 2021 teaching myself the core languages of the web (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript), and then completed an immersive Software Engineering program with General Assembly which lasted from September through December of 2021. I've been learning and coding at least a few hours a day ever since!

Q: What's your favorite coding language?

A: Definitely JavaScript. There's just so much you can do with it, even outside a web browser. It was the first scripting language I studied, and I find myself "thinking in JavaScript" to work through problems even when writing code in other languages like Python.

Q: Why should I hire you?

A: Although I don't currently have the highly sought-after "3 to 5 years of industry-specific experience", I'm dedicated to my craft, intelligent enough to succeed in this industry, and determined to get just a little bit better every day. As a bonus, I'm a loyal team player, I'm easy to work with, and I'll brighten your day with quips and amusing personal anecdotes.
